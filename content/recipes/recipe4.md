{{< ingredients >}}
- 200g cream cheese
- 100g sugar
- 1 egg
- 100g biscuit crumbs
- Butter
{{< /ingredients >}}

{{< instructions >}}
1. Mix biscuit crumbs with melted butter and press into a pan.
2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and egg until smooth.
3. Pour onto the base and bake at 180Â°C for 25 minutes.
4. Chill and serve.
{{< /instructions >}}
